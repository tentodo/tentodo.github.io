Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is Ten Todo?

Ten Todo aims to arrange your daily work and life through intellective arithmetic, in order to help you manage your time more effectively and improve your productivity. Every day, Ten Todo will provide you a todo list which includes 10 items, since the academic analysis prove that people can finish no more than 10 things in one day with highest productivity. Under the help of Ten Todo, you can balance your work and life, at the same time be more efficient.

Q2: How does Ten Todo arrange the sequence？

Ten Todo arranges the sequence of your schedules according to two index, important and urgent. By using these tow index, we can divide all thing into 4 fields:
Q1 important and urgent, crises, pressing problems, deadline-driven projects;
Q2 important and not urgent，Prevention activities, relationship-building, recognizing new opportunities, planning;
Q3 not important and urgent，interruptions, some calls, some mail, reports, meetings, proximate pressing matters;
Q4 not important and not urgent，trivial, busy work, some mail, calls, time wasters. 
Importance is shown as priority, you can judge the priority of the items by your owe objective opinion, while urgent is  shown as deadline, that’s subjective end day of the item. According to your objective priority and subjective end day, Ten Todo will calculate a synthetical score, which finally will be use to arrange the sequence.

Q3: Is there any functions in the future?

There will be three functions in the future to satisfied users demand:
a. Guide. Items and informations through any other entrance, while you can also export any items and information from Ten Todo.
b. Invitation. You can invite your friend to finish items together with you, also you can share your items to related people. When you invite your friend to one item, this item will show on his/her lists automatically. When you share your inters to other people, they can see all your list by day/week/months.
c. Location-based notification. Ten Todo will record your location of items and when you near by, it will notice you to finish items.
